♻️ 1. Reduce, Reuse, Recycle

Why toss it when you can reuse it?
  • • Bring your own water bottle, lunchbox, and tote bag.
  • • Recycle paper, plastic, and even old electronics.
  • • Got half-used notebooks? Use the blank pages!

💡 2. Save Energy Like a Pro

Small changes = big impact.
  • • Flip off lights and screens when you're done.
  • • Switch to LED bulbs if you can.
  • • Unplug chargers when not in use — they still suck power!

🚲 3. Travel the Green Way

Skip the car when you can.
  • • Walk, bike, or hop on public transport.
  • • Carpool with friends — it’s eco-friendly and fun.
  • • Short trip? Grab your bike instead of a ride.

🥗 4. Eat with the Planet in Mind

Your food choices matter.
  • • Try more plant-based meals (they’re tasty, promise).
  • • Shop local and organic when possible.
  • • Say no to plastic-wrapped everything.

💻 5. Go Digital

Save trees and stay organised.
  • • Submit assignments online.
  • • Take notes on your laptop or tablet.
  • • Use e-books and digital tools for studying.

🛍️ 6. Shop Smart

Support brands that care.
  • • Look for eco-friendly companies.
  • • Choose items with less packaging.
  • • Check for labels like Fair Trade or FSC.

🌱 7. Get Involved

Be the change on your campus.
  • • Join (or start!) a sustainability club.
  • • Help out at clean-up events or tree plantings.
  • • Push for greener policies at your school.

📣 8. Spread the Word

Inspire other students to go green.
  • • Share tips on social media – see mini webpage example (Naksdeep, Scarlett and Monroe)
  • • Talk to friends and family about sustainability.
  • • Host a workshop or awareness event.
